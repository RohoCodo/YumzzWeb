How does our service work?
Users in the local area are able to see restaurants
partnered with Yumzz on our map view. From there,
the user can see the information on the restaurant,
the restaurant's menu and details for each dish.
The user can either use our digital menu or scan
the restaurant's menu to see info on a specific dish.
If the user decides to order one of the dishes,
they can click the order button. If the restaurant
has chosen to connect their POS system to our app,
then the order goes directly to their ticketing system.
If not, then the user must order as normal.
Our own POS system is being developed and will be
offered in a month.
